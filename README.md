Nebula Contacts Manager v1.3.6 PRO
Nebula is a professional, high-performance contact management system designed for speed, privacy, and absolute control over your data. Unlike cloud-based solutions, Nebula operates 100% locally on your computer, ensuring your sensitive information never leaves your device.

🌟 Main Features
Privacy-First Architecture: All data is stored in a secure local database. No internet connection is required for operation, and no data is ever tracked or collected.

Modern Adaptive UI: Full support for Dark and Light modes. The interface is designed to be intuitive, clean, and easy on the eyes during long work sessions.

Professional Toolset (PRO):

Advanced Export: Save your entire contact list as professional Excel (.xlsx) spreadsheets or clean PDF reports with one click.

Unlimited Storage: The PRO version removes the 15-contact limit for large-scale management.

Device-Specific Licensing: Secure activation system tied to your unique hardware.

Reliable Data Management:

Smart Backup: Instant backup to your Desktop with an automated shortcut to Google Drive for safe cloud mirroring.

Database Import: Easily move your data between computers or restore from previous backups.

Integrated Communication:

Instant Search: Find any contact in milliseconds using the real-time search filter.

Direct Email: Connect with your contacts instantly via your default mail client.

💻 System Requirements
Platform: Windows 10 / 11.

Portability: Single-file executable — no complex installation process required.

Storage: Minimal disk space (uses a local .db file for data persistence).

🔒 Security & Privacy
Nebula is built on the principle of Zero-Knowledge. The developer has no access to your database, your contacts, or your usage patterns. You are the sole owner and custodian of your data.
